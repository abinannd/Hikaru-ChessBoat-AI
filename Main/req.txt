Phase 9 – Step 3: AI Side Selection

Objective

Allow the player to choose whether to play as White or Black before a new game starts.

The AI should automatically control the opposite side.

Reuse the existing AI integration and move execution pipeline.

Do NOT duplicate gameplay logic.

────────────────────────────────────────────

IMPORTANT

Reuse:

• ChessInference
• ChessBoard
• python-chess.Board()
• Existing AI move execution
• Existing human move execution

Do NOT modify:

• inference.py
• chess_model.py
• board_encoder.py
• move_encoder.py
• train.py
• evaluate.py

────────────────────────────────────────────

Game Mode

Support:

✓ Human (White) vs AI (Black)

✓ AI (White) vs Human (Black)

Store the current mode as:

self.human_color

self.ai_color

Do NOT hardcode colors anywhere else.

────────────────────────────────────────────

User Interface

Add an AI Side selector.

A simple Spinner, DropDown, or two Radio Buttons is acceptable.

Options:

• Play as White

• Play as Black

Changing the selection should affect the next New Game.

Do NOT change the side in the middle of an active game.

────────────────────────────────────────────

New Game

When a new game starts:

Create a fresh python-chess.Board().

Reset move history.

Reset redo stack.

Reset status.

If AI is White:

Immediately trigger the AI's first move.

If Human is White:

Wait for the user's first move.

────────────────────────────────────────────

Turn Logic

The AI should move only when:

board.turn == ai_color

The human may interact only when:

board.turn == human_color

Prevent interaction during the AI's turn.

────────────────────────────────────────────

Status Bar

Display:

White to Move

Black to Move

Include:

Playing as White

or

Playing as Black

Example:

White to Move | Playing as Black | AI Thinking...

────────────────────────────────────────────

Architecture

Create dedicated methods.

Examples:

set_player_side()

start_new_game()

is_human_turn()

is_ai_turn()

Avoid scattering color checks throughout the code.
