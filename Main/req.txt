Phase 5 – Step 9: Evaluation & Benchmarking

Objective:
Evaluate the playing strength and prediction quality of the supervised chess AI.

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT modify the trained model.
Do NOT retrain.
Do NOT change inference logic.

----------------------------------------------------
Create
----------------------------------------------------

Main/src/evaluate.py

----------------------------------------------------
Requirements
----------------------------------------------------

1. Load the trained model using ChessInference.

2. Read a PGN file containing evaluation games.

3. Replay every game move by move.

For every position:

- Generate the AI prediction.
- Compare it to the actual move played.

Record:

- Correct prediction (Top-1)
- Whether the actual move appears in the Top-3
- Whether the actual move appears in the Top-5
- Whether the actual move appears in the Top-10

----------------------------------------------------
Statistics
----------------------------------------------------

Compute:

Total positions evaluated

Top-1 accuracy

Top-3 accuracy

Top-5 accuracy

Top-10 accuracy

Average inference time

Average legal moves

Prediction failures

Invalid positions skipped
